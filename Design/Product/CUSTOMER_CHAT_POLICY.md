# Customer Chat Policy

Status: confirmed product direction. This policy applies to conversations between real customers. Seed conversations remain subject to the additional `SEED_CHAT_VALUE_POLICY.md`.

## 1. Principle
DatingEasy888 supports private, open conversation between consenting adults. The system does not direct what customers should believe, rewrite their messages, insert company responses, or interfere merely because a conversation is unusual, controversial, intimate, political, emotional, or critical of the company.

Customers choose their own topics and may end, mute, block, or report a conversation at any time.

Freedom to converse does not include permission to threaten, exploit, or target other people with racist, discriminatory, or violent abuse.

## 2. Conversations Normally Allowed
Consenting adults may discuss:
- Dating, relationships, affection, and consensual adult intimacy
- Personal opinions and experiences
- Politics, religion, culture, and social issues
- Humor, disagreement, and debate
- News, history, fiction, games, sports, and entertainment
- Difficult emotions and personal problems
- Violence as a subject in news, history, fiction, recovery, or safety discussion

The platform does not remove content solely because an opinion is unpopular or because two adults disagree.

## 3. Prohibited Discriminatory And Racist Conduct
Customers must not:
- Direct racist slurs or degrading stereotypes at another person
- Harass, humiliate, or dehumanize people because of race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or another protected characteristic
- Advocate exclusion, segregation, expulsion, or harm against a protected group
- Repeatedly target a customer with unwanted discriminatory comments
- Use profiles, messages, or media to organize discriminatory harassment

Personal dating preferences are allowed when expressed respectfully. No customer is required to date or continue speaking with another customer. Preferences cannot be used as permission to insult, threaten, or publicly degrade people.

Good-faith discussion about discrimination, identity, history, public policy, religion, or culture is allowed when it is not targeted abuse or incitement.

## 4. Prohibited Violent Conduct
Customers must not:
- Make credible threats of physical harm
- Encourage, praise, request, or coordinate real-world violence against a person or group
- Share instructions intended to facilitate an imminent violent act
- Use violent intimidation, stalking threats, or coercion
- Share graphic violent media to shock, harass, or threaten another customer

Discussion or depiction of violence in news, history, fiction, sport, art, personal recovery, or prevention is not automatically prohibited. Context, target, intent, and credible risk matter.

Content indicating imminent danger may be escalated under the incident and legal-response process.

## 5. Other Non-Negotiable Safety Limits
Even on an adult platform, customers must not use chat for:
- Child sexual abuse material, sexualization or exploitation of minors, or enticement of minors
- Non-consensual intimate imagery or threats to publish it
- Sexual coercion, trafficking, exploitation, or credible threats
- Doxxing or sharing private identifying information to cause harm
- Malware, credential theft, fraud, extortion, or scams
- Repeated unwanted contact after blocking or a clear request to stop

Applicable legal reporting and evidence-preservation duties override normal non-interference.

## 6. Privacy And Limited System Intervention
DatingEasy888 does not promise that chats are invisible to the service or end-to-end encrypted unless that capability is later explicitly designed and verified.

The system may process messages only as needed to:
- Deliver and store the conversation
- Calculate disclosed credit charges
- Detect malware, spam, fraud, prohibited exploitation, and serious safety threats
- Enforce blocks and consent controls
- Investigate a customer report
- Meet legal obligations
- Maintain service reliability and security

Employees and administrators may not browse customer chats out of curiosity or for entertainment. Human access requires an approved support, moderation, safety, security, or legal purpose and must be audited.

Automated systems must not change the meaning of a customer's message. If a message is restricted, the sender receives a clear status and appeal path where appropriate.

## 7. Customer Controls
Customers can:
- Block another customer immediately
- Mute notifications
- Report a profile, message, media item, or conversation
- Withdraw consent for intimate conversation
- End a conversation without explanation
- Review applicable privacy and retention information

A block stops new direct contact through normal customer messaging.

## 8. Moderation Approach
Moderation is based on behavior, context, and risk rather than viewpoint.

Possible actions:
1. No action when content is allowed.
2. Customer-controlled block or mute.
3. Warning and education for a lower-severity first violation.
4. Content or media restriction.
5. Temporary messaging restriction.
6. Account suspension or ban for severe or repeated violations.
7. Emergency or legally required escalation.

Serious threats, exploitation, non-consensual intimate imagery, or child-safety concerns may skip directly to stronger action.

Every human moderation action requires:
- Reason code
- Relevant evidence
- Reviewer identity
- UTC timestamp
- Action and duration
- Appeal status where applicable

## 9. Reporting Reasons
Initial customer report reasons:
- Harassment
- Racist or discriminatory abuse
- Threat or violence
- Unwanted sexual content
- Non-consensual intimate image
- Minor-safety concern
- Scam, fraud, or extortion
- Impersonation
- Privacy or doxxing
- Spam
- Other safety concern

## 10. Product Standard
The desired balance is:

> Private adult conversation by default, narrow intervention for targeted abuse, exploitation, credible harm, security, or legal obligations.

The company must not use moderation to secretly steer customer conversations or favor a viewpoint. It must also not ignore credible harm simply because chat is intended to be private.

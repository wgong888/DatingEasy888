# DatingEasy888 Product Requirements

## Product Name
- Working brand: DatingEasy888
- Working domain: www.DatingEasy888.com
- Previous screenshot/source brands: Horizon Singles, Best Dates
- Brand cleanup needed: replace old references in future design and UI work

## Product Goal
DatingEasy888 is an 18+ social dating and chat platform where adults can create profiles, discover other members, communicate through chat/mail, follow activity, and buy credits for premium interactions.

Once an eligible customer signs in, the customer may immediately use every
enabled action provided by the customer UI without employee or administrator
approval. The server continues to enforce age, balance, consent, block,
rate-limit, account-state, safety, and legal rules.

Customer sessions remain active during meaningful use and automatically log out
after twenty consecutive minutes without customer interaction. Background
updates and incoming notifications do not reset this timer.

## Target Users
- New visitors who want to browse or join an adult dating/social site
- Registered members who want to search, chat, receive messages, and manage their profile
- Paying members who buy credits/gifts or premium interactions
- Admin/company staff who review users, payments, reports, safety events, and platform health

## Core User Value
- Easy registration and profile setup
- Fast discovery of profiles by age, location, online status, interests, and preferences
- Messaging, mail, and chat flows with clear unread counts
- Credit balance and purchase flows that are easy to understand
- Safety controls: block, report, moderation, identity/age verification

## Business Rules
- Users must be 18 or older.
- Users need a profile before they can fully use discovery and messaging.
- Messaging, gifts, or premium actions may consume credits.
- Some message types may be free, depending on promotional or user status rules.
- Photos can be public or private.
- Profile and conversation pictures are processed to a maximum 100 x 100 pixel
  bounding box with preserved aspect ratio and automatic compression.
- Processed pictures use JPG or PNG. Safely readable uploads in other image
  formats are automatically converted; transparency uses PNG and ordinary
  photographs use JPG.
- Conversation voice files are automatically transcoded and compressed before
  acceptance; the final duration and byte limit remain to be approved.
- Admins can suspend, ban, review, restore, or verify accounts.
- `CustomerProfile.Seed` defines three customer types: 0 real, 1
  employee-operated seed, and 2 autonomous robot.
- Real customers may chat with real, seed, and robot customers.
- Seed customers chat only with real customers through assigned employees.
- Robot customers chat only with real customers and respond autonomously.
- Robot customer profiles use the same customer-facing fields and screens as
  real customer profiles. Internal robot classification is admin-only.
- Authorized administrators may edit robot customer profiles.
- Every approved large city has at least six robot customers, including at
  least three Man and three Woman profiles.
- Each robot customer may work no more than eight hours per assigned-city local day.
- Scheduled tasks guarantee at least one Man and one Woman robot online in each
  coverage-ready large city and activate reserves after failures.
- Robot customer scheduling follows `ROBOT_CUSTOMER_WORK_POLICY.md`.
- Conversations between two non-real customer profiles are prohibited.
- All seed customer profiles are generated by AI before product deployment.
- Seed profiles must be original synthetic characters or use content with explicit commercial rights and model consent.
- Real profiles, photographs, biographies, or personal stories must not be copied or lightly altered to create seed profiles.
- Seed generation follows `SEED_PROFILE_GENERATION_POLICY.md`.
- Seed profiles are company-operated profiles whose internal classification is
  not displayed to real customers.
- Human employees and robot employees may operate internally classified seed profiles.
- Seed conversations must provide genuine emotional and conversational value through attentive listening, respectful affection, mood support, relaxation, positivity, fun, humor, and natural continuity.
- A customer's willingness to keep talking must come from conversation quality and free choice, not deception, dependency, guilt, urgency, or spending pressure.
- Seed conversations follow `SEED_CHAT_VALUE_POLICY.md`.
- Real-customer conversations are open and private by default; the system does not steer, rewrite, or suppress lawful adult discussion merely because it is controversial or intimate.
- Racist or discriminatory abuse, credible violent threats, exploitation, and other narrow non-negotiable safety violations are prohibited.
- Real-customer conversations follow `CUSTOMER_CHAT_POLICY.md`.
- Online customer activity is reconciled at least every three minutes for new chats, chat-history presence, favorites, inbox, search results, and profile requests.
- Real-time events may update immediately; the three-minute cycle guarantees reconciliation after missed or delayed events.
- Profile viewers may send Hello, Cuddle, Hug, Flirt, Teasing, or SexRequest interaction requests.
- `SexRequest` is presented as an intimate-chat request and never constitutes consent by itself.
- Online refresh and profile requests follow `ONLINE_REFRESH_AND_PROFILE_REQUEST_POLICY.md`.
- Customer account navigation includes Profile, Visitors, Credits, Settings, Log Out, and Back to Home.
- Eligible full-profile views support a privacy-controlled Visitors list; internal staff/service access and blocked relationships never create visible visits.
- The full profile supports a photo carousel, Message, Favorite, profile requests, About, profile facts, Block, and Report.
- Active conversation detail includes Chats/Mailbox switching, customer
  identity, message history, View Profile, Favorite, safety controls, and a
  cost-aware composer.
- Customers must see applicable credit costs before paid interactions with seed profiles.
- Credits, rewards, consumption costs, and gifts follow `CREDITS_REWARDS_POLICY.md`.
- Successfully sent gifts are final and non-refundable.
- A seed-recipient gift credits 80% to the employee overseeing the seed at gift
  time and preserves that attribution after reassignment.
- Every record-list response contains at most 20 records and exposes `Next` when
  another page exists.

## Design Principles
- Clear, direct, adult-friendly experience
- Mobile-first usability with desktop polish
- Keep the customer interface simple, compact, robust, and easy to learn.
- Support both desktop computers and mobile phones.
- Prioritize quick access to profiles, conversations, credits, and account actions.
- Strong trust and safety cues without making the UI feel heavy
- Simple payments and transparent credit costs
- Avoid confusing overlap between mail, chat, inbox, and notifications

## Frontend And Backend UI Direction

### Customer Frontend
- Mobile-first: phone interaction, layout, performance, and readability are
  the primary design and acceptance target.
- Customers may use the complete service in a supported mobile browser without
  installing an application.
- The mobile website remains a first-class supported product after native iOS
  and Android applications launch; installation is optional and must never be
  required for a core customer workflow.
- Simple and compact
- Robust and reliable
- Easy to use without training
- Responsive on desktop and mobile phones
- Compatible with Edge and Chrome on Windows 10 22H2 and Windows 11
- Compatible with 2020-or-newer Macs running macOS 12 or later, with Edge, Chrome, and supported Safari
- Mobile minimum is iOS/iPadOS 17; supported Android browsers remain included
- Approved public pages support Bing indexing; Bing is a search engine, while Edge is the Microsoft browser target
- Best-effort support for other Unix-like systems with a standards-compliant current browser
- Internet Explorer 11 is not supported because it is retired and lacks required modern security and web capabilities
- Touch-friendly on mobile
- Primary actions must be reachable and comfortable for one-handed phone use.
- Registration, profile, discovery, favorites, chat, gifts, credits, safety,
  and account management must not require a desktop-only step.
- Avoid excessive controls, dense administration tools, and unnecessary decoration

### Customer Mobile Applications
- Dedicated iOS and Android customer applications are planned after the Arfa
  review.
- Customers choose independently between the installed application and the
  mobile website and may move between them with the same account.
- The apps provide the same authoritative customer accounts, profiles,
  conversations, balances, purchases, gifts, safety state, and policies as the
  customer web experience through shared versioned APIs.
- Core customer capabilities remain functionally equivalent in the application
  and supported mobile browsers. Native conveniences such as push notifications,
  biometric sign-in, camera integration, and app shortcuts may differ without
  making the browser path incomplete.
- Marketing and account screens may offer app installation, but they must not
  block, repeatedly interrupt, or degrade continued browser use.
- Mobile apps must not duplicate credit, payment, chat, robot, moderation, or
  eligibility rules in a way that can diverge from the Web Service.
- Native-app technology, store-account ownership, push notifications,
  purchases, release cadence, and minimum OS versions will be approved during
  the post-Arfa architecture review.
- Employee, administrator, CEO, and robot workspaces are not included in the
  customer mobile applications.

### Employee/Admin Backend
- Designed only for large desktop screens
- Convenient and efficient for long work sessions
- Robust and easy to operate
- Information-dense without becoming confusing
- Optimized for simultaneous chats, monitoring, tables, reports, and repeated actions
- No mobile layout is required unless this decision changes later

## Brand And Visual Direction
- DatingEasy888 will reference the existing Horizon Singles and Best Dates screenshot styles.
- Horizon Singles is a little too shiny, bright, and visually intense.
- Best Dates is a little too plain and minimal.
- DatingEasy888 should land between them: warm, confident, polished, social, and easy to use.
- The design should keep the dating/social energy from Horizon while using the cleaner readability and structure from Best Dates.
- Avoid excessive glow, heavy gradients, overly saturated effects, or busy decorative elements.
- Avoid a flat/plain interface that feels unfinished, generic, or low-trust.
- Preferred feeling: friendly, active, trustworthy, adult, and modern.
